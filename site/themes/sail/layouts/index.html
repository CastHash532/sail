Improve Your Team's Workflow

Sail provides a consistent, declarative environment for everyone working on the same project. Spend less time configuring, and more time coding.

  • No more "It works on my machine", everyone working on the same project is working in the same environment.
  • Stop duplicating effort, source-control and collaborate on the environment.
  • Instant set-up, open pre-configured environments straight from GitHub or GitLab.
  • Feel production in your dev env, base your project's Dockerfile on your production image.
